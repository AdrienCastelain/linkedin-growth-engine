# Content Audit Template

> **What this is:** A snapshot of your last 30 days of LinkedIn posts. The workflow uses this to (a) avoid repeating topics you've already covered, (b) feed performance data into next week's batch, and (c) flag content fatigue patterns before they hurt reach.
>
> **How to use it:** Copy this file to `my-content-audit.md` (or wherever you keep your brand assets) and fill in one row per post you've published in the last 30 days. Update it monthly. If you're brand new to LinkedIn and have no posts to audit, mark this as "Week 1 — no audit data" and skip to the workflow.
>
> **Time to fill:** 15-30 minutes for a month of posts. Worth it. Posts you forgot you wrote always come back to bite you.

## Post Inventory

**Columns explained:**

- **Format:** text+image / carousel / poll / video / multi-image
- **Hook style:** story / contrarian / number
- **Closing type:** A (question) / B (incomplete) / C (nudge)
- **Topic:** 5-10 word description
- **Reactions:** Total reactions count
- **Comments:** Total comment count (only count substantive ones — 5+ words)
- **Reposts:** Reshare count
- **Notes:** Anything memorable — DMs received, screenshots taken, lead inquiries attributed
