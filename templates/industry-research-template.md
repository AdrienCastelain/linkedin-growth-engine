# Industry Research Template

> **What this is:** A snapshot of what's happening in your industry right now. The workflow uses this in Step 2 (Topic Selection) to make sure your weekly batch ties into current conversations instead of recycling evergreen takes that feel disconnected from the moment.
>
> **How to use it:** Copy this file to `my-industry-research.md`. Refresh it monthly. It does not need to be exhaustive — 30 minutes of focused research is enough. The goal is "what would my ICP have read this week," not "comprehensive industry analysis."
>
> **Time to fill:** 30-45 minutes per refresh. Block it on your calendar like any other ritual.
