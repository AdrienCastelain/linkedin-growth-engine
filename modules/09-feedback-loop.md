# Performance Feedback Loop

**Source of truth:** `../rulebook/linkedin-growth-engine-rulebook.md` — Section 8
**Load when:** Monthly/weekly reports, strategy adjustments
**Full rules:** For complete rules, read the Rulebook, Section 8.

---

## Quick Reference

- **Monthly analysis:** reactions (by type), comments, reposts, impressions, engagement rate per post
- **Pattern extraction:** best hook style, best closing type, best pillar, best length, best day/time, best format
- **Weight adjustment:** min 3+ posts of same type before adjusting; max 2 weight shifts per month (prevent thrashing)
- **No single hook style or closing type > 65% of batch**
- **Weekly email:** informational preview (not approval gate), silence = autopilot, scannable in <2 minutes
- **Weekly email must include:** hook previews (120 chars), "Why These Posts" with Topic Attribution (pillar, pain point, trend), passive reshare suggestions
- **Monthly report:** lead with insight not number, 2 pages max, no jargon, includes competitive update and next month preview
- **Re-engagement trigger:** 0 comments after 4 hours AND reactions below 30-day average -- comment on 5 larger accounts, add first comment, share to story
- **Low performer tagging:** after 24 hours with 0 comments, tag and extract what was different
