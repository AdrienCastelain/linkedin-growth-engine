## Quick Reference

- **Specificity over generality** -- "57% to 89% in 5 weeks" beats "significant improvement"
- **Earned opinions** -- backed by visible experience, not asserted from authority
- **Nuanced industry commentary with a position taken** -- drives substantive comments
- **Real vulnerability** -- unresolved, messy, uncomfortable (not tidy redemption arcs)
- **Teaching through story** -- show the lesson happening, don't announce it
- **Algorithm rewards (2026):** meaningful comments (15+ words) > saves > all comments > reply chains > dwell time > reshares with commentary
- **Algorithm suppresses:** engagement bait, empty broetry, reaction-only posts, "hide"/"unfollow" triggers, third-party links in body (~25-40% reach reduction)
- **Tone drift red flags:** tone shifts within post, sudden formality increase, generic advice, appended-feeling closing
