# Module 02: Anti-Pattern Blacklist

**Source of truth:** `../rulebook/linkedin-growth-engine-rulebook.md` — Section 2
**Load when:** Writing any content (posts, comments, reshares)

## Quick Reference

- **Tier 1 absolute ban words:** delve, tapestry, paradigm, harness, unlock, realm, meticulous(ly), unparalleled, underscore, showcase, pivotal, testament, commendable, groundbreaking, revolutionize, transformative
- **Tier 2 strong ban words:** leverage, synergy, innovative, game-changer, cutting-edge, landscape, intricate, crucial, vibrant, foster, holistic, garner, accentuate, pioneering, trailblazing, unleash, versatile, redefine, seamless, optimize, robust, breakthrough, empower, streamline
- **Tier 3 (max once/month):** elevate, adaptive, effortless, data-driven, proactive, visionary, disruptive, reimagine, agile, democratize, accelerate, state-of-the-art, dynamic, future-proof, next-generation
- **Banned structural patterns:** broetry (4+ single-sentence paragraphs with no new info), false opposition as crutch, negation-reframe opener ("Not X. Because Y." — max once per post, never in opening lines, never combined with another false opposition), snappy triads, unearned profundity, template scaffold, therapist mode, manufactured vulnerability, borrowed authority (Bezos/Musk/Jobs)
- **Banned CTAs:** "Agree or disagree?", "Comment YES", "Follow me for more", "Thoughts?"
- **Banned formatting:** emoji-prefixed bullets, rocket emoji anywhere, unicode bold/italic, decorative unicode, 3+ trailing hashtags in body
- **Links:** Third-party external links in post body reduce reach 25-40%, move to first comment. LinkedIn-native links are fine in body.
- **Reshares:** Min 50 words commentary, must take a position, must stand alone, max 2/month, never two in same week
- **Em dashes:** Default max 2 per post, calibrate from your reference samples

**Full rules:** For complete rules, read the Rulebook, Section 2.
