# Connection Growth Strategy

For accounts under 5K followers, strategic connection requests have more immediate impact than content optimization. Content quality without audience is a tree falling in an empty forest.

## Target Connection Criteria

| Priority | Who | Why |
|----------|-----|-----|
| **High** | People who engaged with your posts (commented, reacted) | Already warm — highest accept rate |
| **High** | People who engaged with benchmark accounts' posts in your niche | Proven interest in your topic |
| **Medium** | ICP-matching profiles (right title, industry, company size) | Cold but targeted |
| **Medium** | Speakers/attendees at events in your space | Shared context |
| **Low** | Random profiles in your industry | Low signal, low accept rate |

## Connection Request Notes

**Rules:**
- Always include a personalized note (requests with notes: ~50% accept rate. Without: ~30%)
- Max 300 characters (LinkedIn limit)
- Reference something specific: their recent post, a shared connection, a mutual interest
- Never pitch in the request. The note opens a door, it doesn't sell.

**Templates (customize, don't copy-paste):**
```
After they commented on your post:
"Thanks for your comment on my [topic] post — your point about [X] was sharp. Would love to connect."

After you commented on their post:
"Your post about [X] stuck with me — especially [specific detail]. Working in a similar space. Would be good to connect."

Cold but ICP-matched:
"I work at the intersection of [X] and [Y]. Your background at [company] caught my eye. Would love to connect."
```

## Weekly Targets by Growth Phase

| Phase | Followers | Requests/week | Focus |
|-------|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Launch (0-1K) | Sub-1K | 15-20 | Warm connections (engaged with your content or benchmark content) |
| Growth (1K-5K) | 1K-5K | 10-15 | Mix warm + ICP cold |
| Momentum (5K+) | 5K+ | 5-10 | Selective, high-value only |

**Rate limits:** Max 100 requests/week (LinkedIn soft limit). Stay well under at 15-20 max. Spread across the week, not all on Monday.

## Follow-Up After Acceptance

When someone accepts your connection request:
- Wait 24-48 hours (don't DM immediately — looks automated)
- Send a brief follow-up: "Good to connect. [One sentence about what you do]. Let me know if our paths cross on [their topic]."
- Do NOT pitch, send a link, or ask for a call in the follow-up
- If they're a warm lead (from post engagement), reference the original interaction
- **For DM etiquette and conversion rules**, see Module 16 (DM Conversion Playbook). All DM rules from Module 16 apply to connection follow-ups.

## Connection + Content Flywheel

The goal is a virtuous cycle:
1. Post content → attracts engagement
2. Engage with commenters → build relationships
3. Send connection requests to engaged people → grow network
4. Larger network → more impressions on next post → more engagement
5. Repeat

At ~1K followers with content-only growth: ~15-25 new followers/month.
With content + strategic connections: ~80-150 new followers/month.
