# Module 05: Proactive Comment Rules

**Source of truth:** `../rulebook/linkedin-growth-engine-rulebook.md` — Section 4.11
**Load when:** Writing comments on others' posts

## Quick Reference

- **Voice shift:** Casual end of your range. Shorter sentences, contractions always, no polished conclusions. Tone = "colleague at a coffee shop."
- **Length:** 15-60 words. Never a mini-essay.
- **Every comment must do ONE thing:** add a specific experience, respectfully push back, extend the idea in a new direction, or ask a genuine follow-up question. Never just agree.
- **Agreement calibration:** Don't default to agreement as opener, most AI comments start with validation. If you agree, earn it by going somewhere specific. Prefer jumping in over warming up. Across 3-5 comments in a session, max 1 should open with explicit agreement.
- **How to find the angle:** Identify the core claim, ask what they left out or what's the exception, lead with that. The agreement is implicit in the fact you're engaging.
- **Three patterns that work:** New angle (dimension they missed), Reframe (different way to see it), Respectful pushback (disagree on one specific thing).
- **Delete test:** If you can delete the first sentence and the comment still works, delete it.
- **Banned patterns:** "Couldn't agree more", "This resonates deeply", "Thanks for sharing", "Great post!", "Love this perspective", starting with author's first name, mirror-back, forced humility, emoji-only, "Keep it up!"
- **Targeting:** Prioritize 2x-10x follower accounts, posts 5-30 comments, under 2 hours old (same timezone) or under 12 hours (cross-timezone). Skip 100+ comment posts.
- **Timing:** Irregular spacing (median 5 min), max 5 comments/session, never comment and post within same 5-minute window.
