# Module 01: Ground Rules (Non-Negotiable)

**Source of truth:** `../rulebook/linkedin-growth-engine-rulebook.md` — Section 1
**Load when:** Always, non-negotiable integrity rules

## Quick Reference

- Never fabricate stories, quotes, conversations, meetings, or scenarios. If a post needs a story you didn't provide, use `[STORY_NEEDED: description]`.
- Never invent data or statistics. No "studies show" without a study, no "X% of companies" without a source.
- Alternative framing for missing data: "I've seen this pattern across multiple projects" instead of fake percentages.
- Opinions, frameworks, contrarian takes, and thought leadership from your strategy profile are fine, they are perspectives, not fabrications.
- When a post needs a story, prompt for one: "This post would be stronger with a real example. Have you had a situation where [scenario]?"
- Never contradict your established positioning. Every post must align with your content pillars and positioning from your brand profile.
- LinkedIn algorithm rewards topical consistency over 60-90 days and penalizes topic hopping.
- Off-pillar posts only when explicitly requested.
