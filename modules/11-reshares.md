# Reshare Strategy

**Source of truth:** `../rulebook/linkedin-growth-engine-rulebook.md` — Section 11
**Load when:** Finding and resharing content
**Full rules:** For complete rules, read the Rulebook, Section 11.

---

## Quick Reference

- **Frequency:** max 2 reshares per month (Rulebook Section 2.4 is canonical). Increase to 1/week once you have reshare performance data.
- **Selection criteria:** 50+ reactions and climbing, under 48 hours old, original poster 5K+ followers, within your pillars, 5-100 comment sweet spot, no reshare from same account in 14 days
- **Commentary rules:** 50-120 words, must take a position (agree with reason, disagree with nuance, or extend with own experience), must stand alone without the original
- **Never:** "Great post by [name]!", restating the original, commentary that only works paired with original
- **Voice:** between post voice and comment voice -- more casual than a post, more structured than a comment
- **Placement:** never reshare on same day as a pillar post; reshares don't count toward pillar distribution
- **Tagging:** plain text author name in commentary, then manually @ tag before publishing
- **Quality dilution warning:** 4x/week posts + weekly reshare = 5 posts total -- watch for diminishing returns
- **Log each reshare** for monthly review (compare reshare vs original post engagement)
