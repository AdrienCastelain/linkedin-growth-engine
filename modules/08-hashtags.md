## Quick Reference

- **2-4 hashtags per post** (start at 2, test upward -- 5 reads as marketer not thought leader)
- **Mix specificity:** 1 broad (500K+), 1-2 mid-range (10K-500K), 1-2 niche (<10K)
- **Post body:** 0 hashtags preferred, max 2 if highly relevant
- **First comment:** 2-4 hashtags, alongside value-adding content (not hashtag-only)
- **Never exceed 5 total** across post + first comment (spam signal since 2025)
- **Third-party links in body:** ~25-40% reach reduction. First comment still better but gap has narrowed
- **Under 5K followers:** niche hashtags in post body. 50K+: broad hashtags become viable
- **Local markets (NZ/AU/regional):** include 1 local hashtag (#NZTech, #AUStartups) per post -- tiny followings but high signal-to-noise
- **Anti-patterns:** hashtags mid-sentence, branded hashtags nobody follows, trending tags unrelated to content, 7+ total
- **Build a roster of 15-20 hashtags** across pillars, rotate 3-4 per post
