# Module 04: Engagement Architecture

**Source of truth:** `../rulebook/linkedin-growth-engine-rulebook.md` — Section 4
**Load when:** Writing posts, closings, hooks, dwell time, cadence, pillars, cold streaks, batch generation

## Quick Reference

- **Closings (mandatory):** Type A (genuine question), Type B (incomplete statement), Type C (practical nudge). Statement-only endings prohibited. Use 2+ types per 4-post week, never same type 3x in a row.
- **"See more" engineering:** First paragraph = curiosity/tension/incomplete story. Never put conclusion in first 3 lines. Truncation point ~210 chars mobile, ~280 desktop.
- **Dwell time:** Min 150 words, sweet spot 200-350, over 500 max 2/month. Strongest insight in middle third. 2026 algorithm: meaningful comments (15+ words) > saves > all comments > reactions.
- **Cadence:** Default 4 posts/week, data-driven day selection (Tue-Thu + optional as fallback). 8-10 AM in **target audience's timezone** (not yours). Never two posts same day. APAC under 5K followers: default 3/week.
- **Pillar weights:** Expertise 40%, Personal story 30%, Engagement 20%, Promotional 10%. No pillar >50%, promotional never >15%.
- **Batch generation:** Weekly batches of 4, not monthly 16. Fresh context per week. Pass previous week's posts as context.
- **Cold streak (3-4 underperformers):** Double comment volume, reduce to 3x/week, lean into strongest format/pillar, check for pattern drift.
- **Timely insertions:** Replaces next scheduled post, passes per-post gates only, cap 30% of monthly posts.
- **When NOT to post:** Major holidays, natural disasters, industry crises, personal emergencies. Paused posts move to following week.
